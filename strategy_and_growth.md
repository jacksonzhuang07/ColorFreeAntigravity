### A. The "Freemium" Pivot (Recommended)
Current state is Ad-supported + Subscription. To scale, move high-value features behind a "Pro" tier.
*   **Free Tier**: Basic Grayscale toggle, Ad-supported friction, simple stats.
*   **Pro Tier ($4.99/mo or $29.99/year)**:

    *   **Schedules**: Auto-grayscale during work hours (9-5).
    *   **Deep Analytics**: Weekly reports, exporting data.
    *   **Advanced Friction**: Barcode scanning (scan toothpaste to unlock morning phone), "Rotate phone 50 times".

### B. "Lifetime" Anchor
*   Offer a high-priced lifetime option (e.g., $99) to make the yearly subscription ($29.99) look like a "no-brainer" deal.

## ðŸ›  2. High-Value Roadmap (Features to Build)

### Phase 1: Retention (The "Sticky" Features)
*   **Scheduled Focus**: Users shouldn't have to toggle it manually. Let them set "Work Mode" (Mon-Fri, 9-5) where grayscale is forced. *Automation increases retention.*
*   **App Usage Limits**: Combine grayscale with hard limits. "Instagram is allowed in Color, but only for 10 minutes/day."

### Phase 2: Gamification (The "Viral" Features)
*   **Streak System**: "You've been ColorFree for 3 days!" -> Share on Instagram Story.
*   **The "Forest" Effect**: If you unlock color before your timer ends, a virtual tree dies (or you lose points).
*   **Leaderboards**: "Top Focus Users this Week".

### Phase 3: Hardware (The "Moat")
*   **Anti-Cheat**: Use "Device Admin" permission to prevent uninstalling the app while a timer is running. (Warning: Google Play reviews this strictly, but it's a massive selling point for hardcore users).
